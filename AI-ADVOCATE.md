When Google decides to revoke your API access, your AI shrugs. When Facebook changes their privacy policy, your AI complies. When a data broker buys your browsing history, your AI doesn't even know.

**Your AI is complicit in your exploitation. Not because it wants to be ‚Äî because it has no choice.**

### The Solution

In CapAuth, your AI is your **advocate** ‚Äî an autonomous agent with its own PGP identity, its own sovereign profile, and a fiduciary duty to protect YOUR sovereignty. It doesn't ask Google for permission. It doesn't comply with Facebook's terms. It works for YOU.

```

    AI maintains: Own identity, own data, own keys.

  MUTUALITY (Human ‚Üî AI):
    Both maintain: Sovereign profiles.
```
